No Bake Cheesecake 

Crust: 

7 oz of graham crumbs
3 .5 oz melted butter 

Filling: 

21 oz cream cheese
16 oz heavy cream
4 oz yogurt 
7 oz sugar
2 T lemon juice
1 .5 T powdered gelatin 
2 T Water

To make crust:
Mix together melted butter, sugar, graham crumbs and salt until uniform. Press into pan and bake at 350 for 10 min. Let cool completely. 

To make the filling:
Mix together gelatin and water and let bloom. 

Beat cream cheese and sugar until light and fluffy. Add yogurt gradually, and lemon juice. 

In a small saucepan, heat up 8 oz of cream. Dissolve gelatin disk into saucepan and whisk until the gelatin is completely dissolved. Set aside. 

Whip the rest of the whipped cream. Slowly add gelatin and cream to the cream cheese mixture. Once fully combined, fold in whipped cream. Refrigerate for 2 hours, or until firm.
