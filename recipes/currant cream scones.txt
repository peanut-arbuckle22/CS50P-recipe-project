Currant Cream Scones
 
3 .25 c ap flour
.5 c sugar
4 t cream of tartar
2 t baking soda
1 t salt
6 T unsalted butter, cubed, cold
1 c dried currants or raisins
1 .5 to 2 c whipping cream
1 egg, lightly beaten
 
Preheat oven to 400.
Combine flour, sugar, tartar, soda, salt in large bowl. Cut in butter until peasized and mixture is crumbly.
 
Mix in 1 c whipping cream to form soft but not sticky dough. Roll out and brush with beaten egg.
 
Bake 10-15 min, serve warm.
