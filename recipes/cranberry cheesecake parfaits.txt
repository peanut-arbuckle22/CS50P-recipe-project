Cranberry Cheesecake Parfaits
 
Cranberry- fig compote:

.5 c dried mission figs, chopped
.75 c sugar
.75 c water
1 cinnamon stick (or 1 .5 t ground cinnamon)
.5 an orange, zested and juiced
1 T 1t balsamic vinegar
8 oz cranberries
 
Biscoff Cookie crumble:

1 16 oz package biscoff cookies
3 T melted butter
pinch of salt
 
Cheesecake fill:

4 oz cream cheese
2 cups powdered sugar
2 T butter
pinch of salt
.25 t vanilla
.75 c heavy cream, whipped
 
Boil water, take off the heat and add chopped figs and orange juice. Let stand for 10 min. Return pan to medium high heat and add sugar and cinnamon. Bring mixture to a boil and add cranberries. Cook for 10 more minutes or until cranberries burst. Remove from heat and add zest, balsamic and salt. Can make a few days ahead of time.
 
 Place biscoff cookies in food processor until very fine. You can also put cookies in a Ziploc, wrap it with a towel and use a rolling pic to crush the cookies.
Add butter and salt and mix until well combined.
 
In a mixing bowl combine cream cheese, butter and salt. Beat until well combined and slightly fluffy. Add powdered sugar gradually. Add vanilla and fold in whipped cream gently.
 
Layer cookies, cheesecake then cranberry mixture into clear dishes, Should fill 6-6oz cups.  Top with sugared cranberries. Keep refrigerated.
