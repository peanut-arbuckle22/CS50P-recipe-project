Lemon Icebox Pie
 
Crust:
 
1 .5 c graham cracker crumbs
.33 c sugar
6 T melted butter
 
Filling:
 
3 eggs, separated
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
.5 - .75 c lemon juice
zest of one lemon
.25 t cream of tartar
.33 c sugar
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
Mix together graham crumbs, sugar and butter and press into pie crust. Add more butter if mixture is too dry.  Place in freezer while you make the filling.
 
Beat egg yolks, add milk, lemon juice, and zest. Pour into frozen crust. Store in fridge while making topping.
 
Whip egg whites and cream of tartar in stand mixer, and once soft peaks stream in sugar. Top pie with meringue and bake for 12-15 minutes until slightly golden. Let cool and chill for 2 hrs to set.
