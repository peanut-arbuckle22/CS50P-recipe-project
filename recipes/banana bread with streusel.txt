Banana Bread with Streusel
 
Banana bread:
1 .5 c ap flour
1 .25 t baking soda
.75 t salt
1 c brown sugar
.33 c sour cream
.5 stick unsalted butter, room temp
2 large eggs
4 very ripe bananas, mashed or blended
.5 c semisweet chocolate chips
.5 c walnuts
 
Preheat oven to 350. Combine all dry ingredients, whisk and set aside. Cream together butter and sugar.  Add in sour cream, beat until light and fluffy, about 4 minutes. Add eggs one at a time, scraping the bowl after each egg. Add in bananas and fold in flour mix. Place in greased loaf pan.
 
Streusel:
7 T butter, room temp
.75 c flour
.5 c brown sugar
.25 c oats
.33 c chopped pecans
1 .5 T cinnamon
.5 t nutmeg
 
Mix together all dry ingredients besides pecans. Mix in butter with fork, until completely combined. Mix in pecans.  Put on top of banana bread batter. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.
