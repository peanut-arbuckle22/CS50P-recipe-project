Quiche Lorraine
 
.75 lb bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 .25 c shredded swiss cheese
4 eggs
1 .25 c whipping cream
.5 c milk
pinch of nutmeg
1 pie shell
 
Preheat oven to 325.
 
Evenly distribute bacon and cheese over bottom of par-baked pie shell.
 
Whisk together eggs, cream milk and nutmeg until well blended. Pour over bacon and cheese mix.
 
Bake for about 25 min.
