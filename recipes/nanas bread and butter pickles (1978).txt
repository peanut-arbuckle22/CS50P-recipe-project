Nanas Bread and Butter Pickles (1978)
 
4 qts thinly sliced cucumbers
1 qt thinly sliced onions
.5 c salt
5 c vinegar
4 .5 c sugar
1 .5 t turmeric powde
4 t mustard seed
1 t celery seed
1 t ground cloves
 
Place cucumbers and onion in large shallow pan, cover with .5 c salt and ice cubes. Let stand for 3 hours.
 
Boil vinegar, sugar, turmeric powder, mustard seeds, celery seeds and cloves.
 
Drain the cucumbers and add to syrup. Let simmer until barely heated through. Pour into jars and seal.
