Pear Buckwheat Cake
 
1 .5 c almond flour
.5 c buckwheat flour
1 t baking powder
1 t cinnamon
.5 stick butter
.25 c brown sugar
.5 c shredded pears
1 t vanilla
4 eggs separated
 
Preheat to oven 350.
 
In a small bowl sift together all dry ingredients. Grate pear, set aside.
 
Cream butter and brown sugar together. Slowly add yolks until well combined. Gently mix in shredded pear and dry ingredients.
 
Whip egg whites and fold into batter.   
 
Decorate with sliced pears, and bake for 35-45 minutes.
