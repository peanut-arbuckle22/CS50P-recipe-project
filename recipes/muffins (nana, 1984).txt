Muffins (Nana, 1984)
 
2 c flour
.67 c sugar
.75 t salt
3 t baking powder
1 egg (beat well)
.67 c milk
.33 c melted butter
1 c fruit
 
Preheat oven to 375.
 
Mix dry ingredients in large bowl.
 
In another bowl mix together egg and milk, and mix into dry ingredients, until just combined, some lumps are fine.
 
Gently mix in melted butter, making sure to not over mix. 
 
Layer in fruit while spooning batter into pan. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar on top. Bake for 20 min.
