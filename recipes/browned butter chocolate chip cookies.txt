Browned Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies
 
2 sticks butter, browned
1 c brown sugar
.5 c sugar
1 egg
1 egg yolk
1 t salt
1 t baking soda
2 .67 c ap flour
12 oz chocolate chips
 
preheat oven to 350.
 
Heat butter in small saucepan over med-high heat. Once it starts boiling, stir constantly (or else it will bubble over sides and burn) until it turns a golden brown color. Remove from heat and let cool.
 
In a small bowl whisk together flour, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
 
In stand mixing bowl combine sugars and cooled butter. Let mix on low speed for a minute or two, making sure to scrape. Add eggs and mix on medium speed for 15 sec. Let stand for 10 seconds, then mix for another 15 sec until lighter in color.
 
Slowly add flour in 3 additions. Add chocolate chips in the last batch of flour and mix until well combined. 
 
Bake for about 12 minutes.
