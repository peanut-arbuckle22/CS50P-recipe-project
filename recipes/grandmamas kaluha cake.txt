Grandmamas Kaluha Cake
 
For cake:

.25 c kaluha
1 pkg chocolate cake mix
1 small pkg instant chocolate pudding
1 c Wesson oil
.25 c vodka
4 eggs
.75 c water
 
For glaze:

1 cup powdered sugar
2 T butter
2 T water
.25 c kaluha
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
Mix cake mix and pudding mix together. Add in all wet ingredients and mix until no lumps.  Bake in bundt pan for 40-45 min. Let cool.
 
For filling combine butter, water and kaluha in a small sauce pan over medium low heat. Add in powdered sugar. Let cool a little before pouring over cake.
