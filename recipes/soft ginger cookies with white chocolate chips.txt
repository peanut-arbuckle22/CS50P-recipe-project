Soft Ginger Cookies with White Chocolate Chips
 
3 c flour
1 t baking soda
2 t ginger
1 .25 t cinnamon
.25 t cloves
.25 t nutmeg
.25 t salt
.75 c butter, softened
.75 c brown sugar
.33 c molasses
1 egg
2 t vanilla
1 .5 c white chocolate chips
optional:
1 c dried cranberries
1 c chopped toasted walnuts
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
Cream together butter and sugar. Add vanilla and eggs and mix until combined.
 
Whisk together flour, spices, salt and soda. Gradually add to butter and eggs. Add chocolate chips, dried cranberries and walnuts last, mixing until just combined.
 
Bake for 9-10 min.
