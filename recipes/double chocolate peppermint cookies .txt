Double Chocolate Peppermint Cookies 

2 .25 c flour 
? c cocoa 
1 t baking soda 
1 t salt
2 t vanilla
2 eggs 
2 sticks of butter, softened 
1 c brown sugar 
.5 c sugar 
12 oz chocolate chips 
Crushed up candy canes 

Preheat oven to 350. 

Whisk  together flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt, set aside. Beat sugars and butter together until fluffy. Scrape and add eggs one at a time, mix until combined. Mix in vanilla. Gradually add in flour mix, then chocolate chips. Bake for 10-12 min. 

To dip in chocolate melt 1 c of chocolate chips with .5 t coconut oil. Dip cooled cookies and let dry on wax paper. Sprinkle chocolate with crushed candy canes.
