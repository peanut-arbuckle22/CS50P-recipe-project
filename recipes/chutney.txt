Chutney
 
4 mangos peeled and diced
1 tsp salt
1 tbs chili peppers
 
Mix and let stand over night.
 
Cook until mangos become transparent about 15 min. Add the following:
 
1 lb golden raisins
1/8 lb (3-4) sliced onions
.5- 1 c citron
.5 c fresh ginger root
2 .5 c vinegar
4 .5 c white sugar
1 .67 c brown sugar
.5 t chopped garlic
.5 t cinnamon
.5 t nutmeg
.5 t all spice
 
Cook until thickened 2-4 hrs.
