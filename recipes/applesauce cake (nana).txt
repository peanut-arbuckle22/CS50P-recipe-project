Applesauce Cake (Nana)
 
1 .5 c applesauce
.5 c butter
1 c sugar
1 c raisins
.5 t salt
2 c flour
2 t baking soda
1 t cinnamon
1 t nutmeg
.5 t clove
1 t vanilla
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
In a small bowl, sift together flour, soda and spices.
 
Melt applesauce, butter, sugar, raisins and salt together over hot water.
 
Take off heat and add dry ingredients to melted mixture, add vanilla.
 
Bake for 40 min, turning half way through.
 
When cooled frost with cream cheese frosting.
