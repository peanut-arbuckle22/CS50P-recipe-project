Peach Cobbler (Miladeen Dennie)
 
.5 c sugar
.5 c flour
.5 c milk
1 t baking powder
.5 t salt
.5 stick butter, melted
lg. can of peaches
 
Preheat the oven to 400. Place melted butter in dish. Mix together flour, salt and powder, then milk.
 
Pour batter over butter, then peaches on top.
Cinnamon, nutmeg and all spice
Sprinkle 2 T sugar over peaches and bake for 30 min.
