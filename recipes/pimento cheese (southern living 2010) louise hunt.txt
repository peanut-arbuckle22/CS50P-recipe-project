Pimento Cheese (Southern Living 2010) Louise Hunt
 
1 .5 c mayo
1 (4 oz) jar of pimento, drained
1 t Worchestire sauce
1 t finely grated onion
.25 t ground red pepper
1 c chopped pecans (chopped, toasted)
8 oz extra sharp cheddar, shredded
8 oz sharp cheddar, shredded
 
Mix together mayo, pimento, worchestire sauce, onion and pepper. Add shredded cheese and cooled pecans to mayo mix.
