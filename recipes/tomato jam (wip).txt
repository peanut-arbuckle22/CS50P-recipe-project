Tomato Jam (WIP)

5 Lg tomatoes 
1 c sugar 
Pinch of salt 
Freshly ground black pepper
1 t balsamic vinegar

Boil large pot of water. Place tomatoes on boiling water for 30-45 sec. Remove with a slotted spoon and let cool. When cool, cut tomatoes into .5 in pieces. Empty water and place cut tomatoes into pan with sugar, salt and pepper. Cook on medium heat, mixing occasionally until tomatoes are broken and soft. Turn heat to low and cover and cook for 10 min.
