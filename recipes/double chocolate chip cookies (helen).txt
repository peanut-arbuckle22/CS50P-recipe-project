Double Chocolate Chip Cookies (Helen)
 
2 .25 c flour
.67 cocoa
1 t baking soda
.5 t salt
1 c softened butter
.75 c sugar
.67 c brown sugar
1 t vanilla
2 eggs
12 oz chocolate chips
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
Combine flour, cocoa, salt, and soda in a small bowl, set aside.
 
Cream butter and sugar together until fluffy. Add vanilla, and one egg at a time.
 
Scrape bowl and gradually add flour mix with mixer on low. Add chocolate chips mixing until just incorporated.
 
Bake for 9-10 minutes.
