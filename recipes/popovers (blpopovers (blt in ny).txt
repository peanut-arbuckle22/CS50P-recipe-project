Popovers (BLPopovers (BLT in NY)
 
4 c milk warmed
8 eggs
4 c flours
1 .5 T salt
2 .25 c grated gruyere cheese
 
Place popover pan in oven. Preheat to 350.
 
Warm milk in small sauce pan. Whisk eggs until frothy and slowly mix into milk. Add in sifted flour and salt mix until mostly smooth.
 
Remove pan from oven and fill each popover cup .75 full (batter should still be warm) and top with 2 .5 T gruyere.
 
Bake 350 for 50 min, turning pan 15 min through.
