Praline Chocolate Pecan Pie
 
2 eggs
.25 cornstarch
1 c sugar
.5 c butter
2 oz praline liqueur
1 cup chopped pecans
6 oz chocolate chips
1 pie crust
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
Beat eggs slightly. Mix together cornstarch and sugar and add to eggs, mixing well.
 
Stir in melted butter and liqueur. Pour into unbaked pie shell.
 
Add pecans and chocolate chips. Spoon 1 T liqueur over top and bake for 45-50 min.
