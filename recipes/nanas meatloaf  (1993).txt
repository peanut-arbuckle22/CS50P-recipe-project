Nanas Meatloaf  (1993)
 
.5 c breadcrumbs
3 T parmesan cheese
1 .25 lb ground beef
.25 c parsley flakes
1 egg beaten
.5 can tomato soup, divided
1 sm onion, chopped
.5 bell pepper
2-4 bacon strips, to top
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
Mix together bread crumbs, cheese, beef and parsley. Add in egg, .5 of the soup, onion and bell pepper and mix until well combined.
 
Form loaf shape and pour rest of tomato soup on top. Place bacon strips on top and bake for 45-50 minutes. Broil for 5-10 min to crisp bacon.
