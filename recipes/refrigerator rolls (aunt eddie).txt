Refrigerator Rolls (Aunt Eddie)
 
2 cups warm water
2 packages of dry yeast
.5 c sugar
8 tablespoons melted butter
5 cups flour
1 T baking powder
.25 t baking soda
1 .5 t salt
 
Mix water, sugar and butter together and sprinkle yeast on top and mix in. Let yeast bloom for about 10 minutes.
 
Mix flour, leavening and salt together and add to yeast mix and mix until well combined. Put in fridge until it has doubled in size.
 
Portion dough and knead into rolls. Place in greased pan and let them rise to double the size.
 
Bake at 450 until done. Brush with melted butter.
