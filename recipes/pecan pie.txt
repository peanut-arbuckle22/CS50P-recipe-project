Pecan Pie
 
.5 c butter, melted
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
.75 c light karo
.75 c dark karo
1 t vanilla
1 c pecans
1 T cornmeal
1 pie crust
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Add in sugar, syrups and cornmeal. Take off heat and let cool.
 
Mix in eggs, being careful to not let them cook. Add vanilla and pour into pie crust.
 
Add pecans and bake for 45-55 minutes.
