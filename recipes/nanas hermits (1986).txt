Nanas Hermits (1986)
 
.75 c butter
1 c sugar
1 egg
.25 c molasses
2 .25 c flour
2 t baking soda
1 t cinnamon
.75 t cloves
.5 t ginger
.25 t salt
.5 c raisins
 
Preheat oven to 375.
 
Sift together flour, soda, salt and spices.
 
Cream butter and sugar together. Add egg and molasses, and mix until blended. Slowly add in flour mix, then raisins.
 
Roll into small logs, press down with fork and sprinkle with sugar. Bake for 7-9 min.
