Pumpkin Bread
 
3 c flour
3 c sugar
2 t baking soda
1 t salt
1 T cinnamon
1 T nutmeg
.5 t ginger
1 cup oil
4 eggs
.67 c water
24 oz canned pumpkin
 
Preheat oven to 350.
 
Whisk together flour, sugar, soda, salt and spices. Form a well in a large bowl, add oil, eggs, water and pumpkin. Beat with wooden spoon until smooth. Makes 2 (or 3 small) loaves.
